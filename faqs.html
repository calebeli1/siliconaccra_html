Our most commonly asked questions:

  1. What is Silicon Accra

    2. It is a 60-acre, master-planned, mixed-use, “walkable” city designed with the objective of promoting collaboration,cohesion and creativity and anchored in creating a city, a fund and a community.

    Silicon Accra is a tech focused project which aims to become Africa's global technology gateway, facilitating the local economy by nurturing ICT industry startups and entrepreneurs; driving collaboration by pooling resources, creating business opportunities and accelerating technology adoption through strategic initiatives and partnerships.

  2. Who owns Silicon Accra?

    3. Silicon Accra is a subsidiary of Twelve Springs Investment Group, an investment project focused on shaping the future of Sub-Saharan Africa through world-class real estate and technology.

  3. What courses does Silicon Accra Innovation School offer?

    4. We are currently focused on Data Science, but will be expanding the scope to other digital and programing fields such as VXF, Digital Marketing, Software Engineering, Programing in React plus so much more.

  4. Where is Silicon Accra located?

    5. Silicon Accra project is a 60-acre, master-planned, mixed-use, “walkable” city located at East Legon Hills, Santeo in Accra.
