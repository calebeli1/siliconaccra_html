Introducing the Silicon Accra Innovation School Startup Fund

We believe that innovation knows no boundaries, and that includes financial constraints. The Silicon Accra Innovation School Startup Fund is our commitment to removing financial barriers and providing the resources needed to turn exceptional ideas into thriving startups.

Why Choose Our Startup Fund?

  1. By Students, for Students:

    We understand the unique challenges and opportunities students face in the tech world. Our Startup Fund is exclusively for Silicon Accra Innovation School students, ensuring that your peers and mentors are here to support your journey.

  2. Seed Capital:

    Every great startup needs a strong foundation. We provide seed capital to help kickstart your venture, whether it's for product development, market research, or other essential needs.

  3. Mentorship:

    Access to seasoned mentors is invaluable. Our network of industry experts and successful entrepreneurs will guide you through the early stages of your startup, offering insights, advice, and networking opportunities.

  4. Resources:

    eyond financial support, we offer access to our cutting-edge facilities, tech resources, and a supportive community of fellow innovators. Your success is our priority.

How It Works

  • Apply:

    Share your tech startup idea with us through our application process. We're looking for innovative, scalable, and impactful projects.

  • Pitch:

    If your idea stands out, you'll have the opportunity to pitch it to our selection committee. We want to hear your vision and passion.

  • Funding:

    If selected, you'll receive the seed capital needed to get your startup off the ground. We'll work with you to ensure the funds are used effectively.

  • Mentorship:

    Throughout your startup journey, our mentors will provide guidance, support, and connections to help you succeed.

  • Launch:

    With the support of Silicon Accra Innovation School's Startup Fund, you'll be well-equipped to launch your tech startup and make your mark in the industry.
